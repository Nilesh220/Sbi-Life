Phase Roadmap

How IdeationX
Works

August 2026 · Phase 1

Campus Induction

SBI Life Insurance officials visit 200 partner B Schools to conduct Induction Sessions introducing the competition, themes, evaluation criteria, and guidelines.

September 2026 · Phase 2 — ACTIVE NOW

Campus Participation & Registration

Participants choose one of the five challenge themes and register as teams of 3. Target scale: 300K Student Reach across 200 B Schools, yielding 50,000+ entries.

You are here
October 2026 · Phase 3

B School Level Evaluation

Faculty panels at each B School evaluate submissions based on Creativity, Feasibility, Research, Social Impact, and Presentation. Top 3 teams per B School advance (600 Qualifiers).

November 2026 · Phase 4

National Qualifier Round

The 600 B School Winners' submissions are evaluated by the central SBI Life Jury Panel. Top 30 entries advance to the Semi-Finals.

December 2026 · Phase 5

Semi-Finals (Virtual)

The Top 30 teams present before the Jury, focusing on depth of research, implementation, innovation, scalability, and Q&A. Top 10 qualify for the Grand Finale.

January 2027 · Phase 6

Grand Finale 🔒 Coming Soon

The Top 10 finalists present before the SBI Life Leadership. Winners receive cash prizes, PPI offers, internships, and mentorship.

Who Can Apply

Eligibility & Rules

🏫

Partner B Schools Only

Open to students enrolled in any postgraduate course at the 200 official IdeationX 2026 partner B Schools across India.

👥

Teams of 3 Members

All entries must be submitted as teams of exactly 3 students. Cross-specialisation and cross-year teams are permitted. Cross-B School teams are not eligible.

🎯

250 Entries Per B School

Each partner B School can submit a maximum of 250 team entries. Once the B School cap is reached, the submission portal locks for that B School.

📋

One Theme Per Entry

Each team must select exactly one of the 5 challenge themes. A student cannot be part of more than one team. Original ideas only — no resubmissions from prior editions.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Can a student from a non-MBA course participate?
+
Yes! IdeationX 2026 is open to students of all postgraduate courses at the 200 partner B Schools. Cross-specialisation teams are encouraged.
What is the submission format?
+
Teams submit a 3-minute video pitch + an 8-10 slide business plan deck. Both must be uploaded before the B School's submission deadline. Templates are available in the Resource Library.
Can I change my team members after registration?
+
Team changes are allowed up to 7 days after registration closes. After that, the team composition is locked for the evaluation. Contact support@ideationx.sbilife.co.in for change requests.
How is B School-level evaluation scored?
+
Faculty panels use a standardised rubric: Problem Clarity (25%), Originality of Solution (25%), Bharat Relevance (20%), Feasibility (20%), and Presentation Quality (10%). Internal scores are not published.
Is there a registration fee?
+
No. IdeationX 2026 is completely free for all participating students. SBI Life bears all competition costs.
How will the Semi-Finals be conducted?
+
Semi-Finals will be conducted virtually. Presentation schedules and instructions will be communicated directly to the 30 shortlisted teams after National Qualifier results in November 2026.
