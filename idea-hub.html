Problem Statement Library

An ever-growing library of real problems. Use these as research starting points, not copy-paste statements.

🏪 Kirana to Coverage

The Trust Gap in Rural Insurance Distribution

Only 3.5% of rural Indians have life insurance. An agent-to-population ratio of 1:1,200 means most families have never had a meaningful insurance conversation. Who do they trust more than any bank or company?

Research Problem
📱 GenZ Protection

The First Salary Moment

India's 22-year-olds are receiving their first salary credits via UPI. 94% spend on consumption in the first 30 days. 0.4% purchase any form of insurance. What would make the protection decision feel as simple and as fast as recharging a phone?

Product Problem
🌾 Climate & Farmer

The Debt-Death Spiral

Average Indian farmer debt: ₹74,121. 42% of farmer suicides are linked to debt. Life insurance penetration in farming communities: under 2%. The solution may not need to look like traditional insurance at all — it needs to fit the farming financial cycle.

Social Problem
🚗 Invisible Workforce

Platform as Insurer

Zomato has 400,000 delivery partners. None are employees. All are contractors. Platforms know their income patterns, work frequency, and location data better than any bank. Could platforms become the distribution backbone for micro-life insurance?

Tech Problem
👩‍💼 Women Architects

Invisible Contribution, Invisible Protection

India's 200 million homemakers contribute an estimated ₹23 lakh crore in unpaid economic value annually. Only 8% have any form of life insurance. If their economic contribution were insured, what would that look like?

Social Problem
📊 Cross-Theme

The Claim Experience Crisis

43% of life insurance claims in India are delayed by more than 30 days. The experience of claiming during grief determines whether the next generation buys life insurance. How would you redesign the claims process from first principles?
